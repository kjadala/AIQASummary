06 — Actions

What I'd do next

The adoption is impressive; the reporting just needs to catch up. In priority order:

1

Standardize the metrics

Convert the before/after hours already in the sheet into the empty numeric columns, so the program has one auditable, roll-up-able time-saved figure.

2

Fill the governance columns

"Support needed" and "Next 60–90 day plan" are effectively blank — capture them so the tracker drives action, not just status.

3

Solve the shared blockers centrally

Tackle token cost as a platform problem and publish a complex-test-case playbook that several teams are independently struggling with.

4

Finish the last partial row

Resident Solutions has filled its placeholders — now complete the D&A "LRO" entry, which lists four agents but still has no stage or before/after, so the portfolio reads cleanly.

5

Build the ROI roll-up

Even a rough tally of documented savings points to very large aggregate hours — formalize it into the number that lands with executives.
